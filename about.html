The Story

Chazel

Crazy Thinker, Full-Time Memelord

IRADUKUNDA Chalom, popularly known as Chazel Memelord, is a Burundian Memelord and Content Creator recognized for creating entertaining and engaging digital content.

He started his content creation journey in 2021 simply for fun, mainly by creating memes, which quickly became the foundation of his brand and the reason many people first discovered his work.

As time went on, he expanded his creativity beyond memes by producing different types of videos, combining entertainment with relatable everyday situations, humor, and sometimes meaningful messages that connect with audiences.

What started as a passion gradually evolved into a full-time profession. Today, through creativity, consistency, and originality, Chazel Memelord continues building a strong digital community while doing what he is known for best: making people laugh and creating content that leaves an impact.
